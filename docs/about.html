Project Details

Project Title

PeerShare

Description

PeerShare is a collaborative research platform designed to facilitate seamless sharing, editing, and collaboration among researchers. It provides a user-friendly interface that enables researchers to connect with each other, exchange messages, share files, and work together on research papers

With PeerShare, researchers can join a shared workspace where they can communicate with their peers in real-time. The platform allows them to send messages, discuss ideas, and provide feedback on research topics. Researchers can also share files, such as research papers, datasets, or reference materials, directly within the platform.

Technologies Used

  • CSS
  • JavaScript
  • Flask
  • Flask-SocketIO
